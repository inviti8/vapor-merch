## The Lore

Compounded by Madame Périgord, widow, amateur botanist, and (it was alleged)
occasional consort of ghosts. Indicated for the chronically wakeful.

Three drops beneath the tongue at midnight, the label once read — though our
legal counsel has since required us to print <em>"do not drop under tongue"</em>
beside it in very small letters.
