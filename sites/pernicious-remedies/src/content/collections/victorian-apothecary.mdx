The Victorian Apothecary line draws from the golden age of medical charlatanism:
a time when a small brown bottle, an ornate label, and a plausible middle name
could cure you of almost anything — for the duration of the placebo effect.

Each bottle in this collection is sold empty and entirely without therapeutic intent.
