There are, by our count, more than enough businesses in the world. We have
nevertheless decided to add one.

Perni Cious Remedies exists because somewhere between the candle shop and the
gothic bookstore, a very particular kind of person is looking for a bottle
labelled <em>Unsolicited Opinions Suppressant</em> to put on their shelf.
We have met that person. We are, in many cases, that person.

Every bottle we sell is printed in a small studio, sealed with a cork, and
shipped in kraft paper with a warning. Nothing inside is real, in a medical
sense. Everything outside is made with earnest attention to the craft.

If any of this appeals to you, we recommend signing up for the list below.
We'll only write to you when something new has been compounded.

— The Proprietor
