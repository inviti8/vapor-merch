## Photography notes

- Shoot on dark stone or wood surface, single-source moody lighting
- 3:4 portrait aspect for product hero, 1:1 for gallery
- JPG quality 82 is fine; optimize with Squoosh or sharp later
- Aim for ~250–500 KB per image at ~1800px wide
